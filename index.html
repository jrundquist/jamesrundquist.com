What I Do

^

Web Development, Design, UX, etc....

In 2008, while a senior in high school, I got my first internship as a developer at What's Up Interactive in Atlanta. Now I primarily work on my own projects along with occasional contract work for companies including What's Up Interactive, Ninja Multimedia, and ProExel.

I began working on back-end development, with PHP and MySQL. Since then I have slowly transitioned to working on front end. With all the new advancements in HTML, CSS and Javascript there was no way I could pass up an opportunity like that.

How and why people using things, especially technology, has always interested me. You have to understand why users do what they do, what they expect things to do, and how to make the product so it meets the users expectations in order to even have a chance at a usable product. I try hard to make sure I know what they needs and habits of the users are at every stage in development of a project.
